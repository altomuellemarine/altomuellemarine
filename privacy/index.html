Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

1. Who we are

This Privacy Policy explains how CONCHA EXPORT LLC, operating commercially as Alto Muelle Marine, collects, uses, stores and deletes information related to our website, commercial services, Instagram Business, Facebook and WhatsApp Business communications.

Website: https://altomuellemarine.com/
Contact email: info@conchaexport.com

2. Information we collect

We may collect information that a person voluntarily provides when contacting Alto Muelle Marine or interacting with our connected business channels.

  • Contact details such as name, email address, phone number, Instagram username, Facebook profile identifier or WhatsApp phone number.
  • Message content sent to Alto Muelle Marine through WhatsApp Business, Instagram Direct, Facebook or website links.
  • Commercial context shared by the user, such as desired boat type, budget, location, import goals, inspection requests, preferred schedule and related questions.
  • Technical metadata required to receive and manage messages, such as message IDs, timestamps, sender identifiers, conversation status and platform source.
  • Website analytics and basic device/browser information when a person visits our website.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data unless the user voluntarily includes it in a message. Users should avoid sending unnecessary sensitive information through chat channels.

3. Data from Instagram, Facebook and WhatsApp

When a user contacts Alto Muelle Marine through Instagram, Facebook or WhatsApp, we may receive data made available by Meta Platforms and WhatsApp Business tools, including the sender identifier, public profile or business messaging information, message text, attachments sent by the user, timestamps and conversation metadata.

We use this information only to manage the commercial conversation, understand the customer's request, provide import advisory support, prepare follow-up and improve internal sales assistance.

4. Use of artificial intelligence

Alto Muelle Marine may use artificial intelligence tools to assist the commercial team. The current use is internal support only.

  • The AI may classify conversations, summarize context, identify customer intent and prepare suggested replies for the team.
  • The AI is not currently configured to automatically respond to customers without human review.
  • Human team members remain responsible for reviewing communications and deciding what to send to the customer.

The purpose of AI assistance is to improve response quality, organization and follow-up. It is not used to make legal, financial or automated eligibility decisions about a customer.

5. How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries about boat import advisory services, personalized USA searches, inspections and logistics.
  • To prepare quotes, commercial follow-up, booking context and customer support.
  • To organize conversations received from Instagram, Facebook and WhatsApp Business.
  • To maintain business records related to active customer requests.
  • To comply with applicable platform rules, legal obligations and security requirements.

6. Data retention and deletion

Messages and related conversation data may be stored temporarily while Alto Muelle Marine handles the customer's request, prepares suggested responses, follows up on the commercial process or maintains records needed for service delivery.

When the data is no longer needed for these purposes, it may be deleted or anonymized, unless Alto Muelle Marine must retain limited information for legal, accounting, dispute resolution, security or operational reasons.

Users can request deletion of their Instagram, Facebook or WhatsApp-related data by following our Data Deletion Instructions or by emailing info@conchaexport.com.

7. Cookies and analytics

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies for basic site functionality, traffic measurement and performance analytics. These technologies help us understand how visitors use the website and improve the experience.

Users can control cookies through their browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

8. Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information only when needed to operate our services, such as with technology providers, hosting providers, messaging platforms, payment/booking services, professional advisors or logistics/service partners involved in a customer-requested process.

Service providers are expected to process information only for the purposes required to provide services to Alto Muelle Marine.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction. No system can be guaranteed to be completely secure, but we work to limit access to information to authorized personnel and necessary service providers.

10. User rights

Depending on the user's location and applicable law, users may have the right to request access, correction, deletion, restriction or portability of their personal information, or to object to certain processing activities.

Requests can be sent to info@conchaexport.com. We may need to verify the requester's identity before processing a request.

11. GDPR and CCPA/CPRA

Where applicable, Alto Muelle Marine will process personal information in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act and California Privacy Rights Act (CCPA/CPRA), and other applicable privacy laws.

We process information for legitimate business purposes, to respond to user requests, to provide requested services, to comply with legal obligations and, where required, based on user consent.

12. Meta Platform compliance

Alto Muelle Marine intends to process Meta Platform data only as described in this Privacy Policy, in our Data Deletion Instructions and in accordance with applicable Meta Platform Terms, Developer Policies, WhatsApp Business terms and applicable law.

We provide a public deletion path for users and will take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize user data received through Meta technologies when a valid deletion request is received, subject to legitimate retention obligations.
