Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: July 3, 2026

How to request deletion

Users can request deletion of personal data related to messages or interactions with Alto Muelle Marine through Instagram, Facebook or WhatsApp Business.

  1. Send an email to info@conchaexport.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  3. Tell us which channel you used: Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp or website.
  4. Include enough information to identify the conversation, such as your Instagram username, Facebook profile name, WhatsApp phone number or email address used to contact us.
  5. Do not send passwords, government IDs or unnecessary sensitive information.

What we will delete

After we verify the request, Alto Muelle Marine will take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize personal data stored in our systems that is associated with the identified Instagram, Facebook or WhatsApp conversation.

This may include message content, conversation summaries, internal AI classification notes, suggested replies and related conversation metadata stored for commercial follow-up.

Information that may be retained

Some information may be retained when necessary for legal, accounting, tax, security, dispute resolution, fraud prevention or legitimate operational purposes. If we cannot delete specific information immediately, we will limit its use to the required purpose.

Response time

We will review deletion requests as soon as reasonably possible. We may ask for additional information if we cannot identify the account or conversation from the information provided.

Deleting data from Alto Muelle Marine systems does not automatically delete messages stored by Meta, Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp or the user's own device. Users may also need to manage or delete messages directly inside those platforms.
